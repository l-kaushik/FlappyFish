Privacy Policy for Flappy Fish

Last updated: November 2025

Flappy Fish is a simple, offline mobile game created for entertainment purposes only. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Flappy Fish does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. The game does not require internet access, account creation, or permissions related to contacts, location, or media. No analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are used in this app.

2. Local Game Data

The game may store your high score or gameplay progress locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, your saved scores will be permanently deleted.

3. Children’s Privacy

Flappy Fish is suitable for players of all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

4. Changes to This Policy

If we update the game in the future to include online features, advertising, or analytics, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly and made available before any such changes take effect.
